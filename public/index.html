My Notes

A private, encrypted notes app with Google Drive sync — available on Android and macOS.

⬇ Download for Android (.apk) ⬇ Download for macOS (.dmg)

Version 1.3.0  ·  macOS: right-click the app → Open on first launch.

Notes & Editor
✍️Rich text editor — bold, italic, underline, strikethrough, headings H1–H6
🔤Fonts & sizes — system fonts plus Nunito, Pacifico, Roboto Mono, and more
🎨Colours — text colour and highlight colour pickers
📋Lists — ordered, bullet, and checklist support
↩️Undo / Redo — full history in the editor and the macOS Edit menu
🔍Find in note — Cmd+F on macOS to search within the current note
Format Painter
🖌️Select source text, tap the paint-brush icon, then select target text — formatting is applied instantly
🗑️Selecting plain text before activating strips all formatting from the target
🖥️Also accessible via Edit → Copy Formatting in the macOS menu bar
Images & Links
🖼️Gallery picker — insert images from your file system via the toolbar
🖱️Drag & drop — drop image files directly onto the editor (macOS)
📋Clipboard paste — Cmd+V pastes copied images inline (macOS)
🔗Hyperlinks — attach URLs to any text; click to open in the browser
✏️Right-click a link to edit or remove it (macOS); long-press on Android
Organisation
📁Folders — create, rename, and delete. Notes move to inbox when a folder is deleted.
↕️Move to folder (macOS) — drag from the notes list onto a folder, or right-click → Move to Folder
👆Move to folder (Android) — long-press a note and choose Move to Folder
🔎Search — full-text search across all notes by title and body content
📌Notes are sorted by last-modified date, newest first
Google Drive Sync
🔄Automatic sync — changes appear on all devices within seconds
⚡Incremental log — only changed items are downloaded; idle polling is near-zero cost
📶Offline support — notes are cached locally; the app works without a connection
💾Storage warnings — notified when Drive is 90% full or completely full
☁️Data lives in your own Google Drive — never on a third-party server
Privacy & Security
🔒AES-256-GCM encryption — notes are encrypted before leaving your device
🔑Your key, your data — the encryption key is derived from your account and stored only in your Drive
👤Local account option — no Google account required; key derived from your password
🗑️Account deletion — permanently removes all notes and your Drive backup from within the app
🚫No ads, no analytics, no data sharing with third parties
