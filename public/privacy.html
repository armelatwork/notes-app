Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

My Notes ("the app") is a private note-taking application for Android and macOS. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights over your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, the app receives:

  • Your Google account display name
  • Your Google account email address
  • An OAuth access token to access Google Drive on your behalf

The app does not collect any other personal information.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All notes and folders are stored in your own Google Drive account, inside a folder named "Notes app". The app never transfers your notes to any third-party server.

Notes are encrypted before being written to Google Drive using AES-256-GCM encryption. Your encryption key is derived from your Google account ID and stored only in your Drive — no one else can read your notes.

On your device, notes are cached in a local database to allow offline access.

3. How Your Data Is Used

  • Your name and email are displayed in the app's user interface only.
  • Your Google Drive access is used solely to read and write your notes and folders.
  • No data is used for advertising, analytics, or shared with third parties.

4. Google Drive Access

The app requests the drive.file scope, which limits its access to only files that the app itself creates. It cannot read any other files in your Google Drive.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party. Your notes never leave your own Google Drive.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained in your Google Drive for as long as you choose to keep it. You can delete individual notes from within the app at any time.

7. Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from within the app:

  • Open the sidebar and tap your name at the bottom.
  • Select Delete account.
  • Confirm the deletion.

This action permanently deletes all notes, folders, and your Drive backup. It cannot be undone.

8. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@thechaos-mynotes.web.app.
